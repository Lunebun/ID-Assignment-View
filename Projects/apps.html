DP (Design Principles)

In DP (Design Principles) we had a chance to redesign an already existing app's logo and loading page. The logo that I personally went with is Telegram. Here you can clearly see the app logo and splash page design.

The simplistic design and color scheme was chosen in order to fit the original's coloring. The birds are something that I thought would fit the theme of a social media app since it is heavily inspired but Twitter but also Pigeon messengers back in the 1900s.

Moreover, we are also tasked with designing the infographics of the app that we've chosen. This is a completely new task for me but I felt that it is a great chance for me to get familiarised with the apps of Adobe photoshop and illustrator.
